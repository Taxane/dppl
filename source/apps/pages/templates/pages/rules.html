Rules of the League

Each week four predetermined players will play four games and those gamesâ€™ scores will count for an overall record. Which four players play can rotate between weeks.

Every team will finish each week with a four game record (eg 4-0, 3-1, 2-2, etc). League games must be mutually agreed upon as being official before they begin. A games is the best of 3 matches to 11, switching serve on 2.

The official in game rules are those outlined by the ITTF : http://www.usatt.org/rules/index.shtml

Each week, the home team captains will be responsible for reaching out to his/her opponent and scheduling his/her game that week. That captain will also be responsible for updating the schedule with the game scores.

Remember, in game point counts do not matter, simply the overall win record across the four official games per week need to be reported.

Clearly this is a social league and any/all disputes need to be resolved during game play. Should a dispute arise, we would strongly suggest taking a drink before attempting a resolution.
